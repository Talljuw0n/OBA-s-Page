Before You Register

Questions from
the room.

You come in with your idea. You leave with your plan.

JUMP is structured across guided modules where Dr. Olori walks you through the exact lessons she has learned across building twelve businesses and consulting for over 100,000 SMEs. Each module attacks a specific obstacle that keeps founders stuck.

You will work through the costly mistakes most entrepreneurs make and how to avoid them. You will define your Minimum Viable Start, the simplest version of your business you can launch with what you already have. You will validate your idea before you build it. You will set up your Profit Foundation so the business sustains both your vision and your livelihood.

You will learn how to make your first sale, develop your distinctive brand voice, and understand the different physics of scaling from 10 to 100 to 1,000 customers.

Every module ends with execution. You do not just learn. You write, decide, and act inside The JUMP Journal as you go.

You leave with a document, a plan, and a community. Not motivation. Not inspiration. A road.

The room is fully online, which means it is fully accessible. Whether you are in Lagos, Abuja, London, or anywhere else in the world, you can be in this room. All you need is a device, a stable connection, and the decision to show up.

JUMP is delivered through guided video modules you work through alongside the cohort, with your JUMP Journal in hand. You watch, you participate, you build, in real time, with people who are in the exact same season as you.

Come in with your idea, even if it is half-formed. Even if you have been sitting on it for years. Even if you are not sure it is good enough yet.

Bring your JUMP Journal. This isn't optional. This is where every decision, every framework, every action point gets written down. The mind forgets, the pen remembers.

Bring a pen. Bring something to drink. Find somewhere comfortable where you can focus for stretches of time without interruption.

If you already have a business, bring your current numbers: what you are charging, what you are making, what is not working. The more honest you are about where you are, the more the modules can move you.

You do not need to be ready. You just need to show up.
