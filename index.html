Her Story

A life built on purpose & impact

Dr. Olori Boye-Ajayi is a senior African trade and development strategist, capital architect, and execution-focused policy leader with 15+ years of experience mobilizing private and public capital, aligning multi-country stakeholders, and delivering measurable impact across Africa.

An alumna of the University of Oxford and the Blavatnik School of Government, Dr. Boye-Ajayi serves as a Member of the AfCFTA Trade and Industrial Development Advisory Council, where she shapes continental trade strategies and industrial development frameworks.

Founder of Borderless Trade Network and Borderless Trade & Investments, she has structured €500M+ in development funds, raised $3M+ for women-led SMEs, and facilitated market access for 100,000+ entrepreneurs. A MANSA Champion with Afreximbank, she has been honoured by MIPAD and at the UK House of Parliament, championing systems that include, protect, and scale real enterprise.

Dr. Olori's strategic insights have made her a sought-after consultant for international development institutions and multilateral organizations — ensuring programs deliver diversity, inclusion, and maximum impact.

Ministry

The Secret Garden Global

TSG The Love Team

Beyond the marketplace, Dr. Boye-Ajayi serves as the Servant Leader of The Secret Garden Global (TSG) — an interdenominational ministry with a global reach.

TSG's mandate is to equip believers to walk in true dominion and to make real disciples of Christ. Known as The Love Team, TSG is committed to nurturing spiritual growth, empowering believers to live as God's ambassadors, and fulfilling the One Billion Souls mandate.

This vision is expressed through key programs operating across Nigeria, the United Kingdom, Ethiopia, the United States, Kenya, Uganda, and beyond.
