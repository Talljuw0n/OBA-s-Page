My Story

A life built on purpose & impact

Dr. Olori Boye‑Ajayi is a senior African trade and development strategist with 15+ years’ experience mobilising public and private capital, aligning multi‑country stakeholders, and delivering measurable impact across Africa.

A Member of the AfCFTA Trade and Industrial Development Advisory Council, she helps shape continental trade and industrial development frameworks. She is the founder of Borderless Trade Network and Borderless Trade & Investments, through which she has structured €500m+ in development finance, raised $3m+ for women‑led SMEs, and facilitated market access for 100,000+ entrepreneurs.

A MANSA Champion with Afreximbank, she is a trusted adviser to multilateral and development institutions on execution‑focused, inclusive trade and investment programmes.

Initiative

Training to Transaction

Providus Bank ECOWAS Parliament GABA SMEDAN

The Training-to-Transaction (T2T) Market Access Programme is a flagship initiative sponsored by Providus Bank, supported by the ECOWAS Parliament and the Global African Business Association (GABA), and implemented by Duchess Natural Limited and Borderless Trade & Investments, co-led by Dr. Olori Boye-Ajayi.

T2T is built for African SMEs who are ready to move beyond readiness into real commercial transactions. The programme delivers structured guidance across export preparation, trade compliance, buyer access, and trade finance bridging the gap between aspiration and execution in African trade.

500 SMEs Supported
2 Cities · Lagos & Abuja
5 Day Training Phase
50+ Market Pipeline Slots
Programme Focus Areas
Export & Market Readiness Trade Compliance Buyer Access Trade Finance FX Solutions Market Linkage

Up to 50 participating companies will be selected into the U.S., UK, Canada, Caribbean, and Africa market access pipelines based on programme performance and trade readiness.
